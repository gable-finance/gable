Version 1 - 31 October 2023

Gable's first iteration will be released shortly after the Radix Babylon launch.

  • Validator Node

    • A validator node will be deployed that collects staking rewards, and deposits it in a liquidity pool on behalf of the staker.

  • Liquidity Pool

    • The first liquidity pool will collect liquidity via staking rewards. A novel approach to provide liquidity, that enables liquidity providers to leverage on their staking rewards.

  • Flash Loans

    • The first offered lending product will be flash loans issuing XRD. Flash loans offer instant liquidity and the ability to lower spreads across exchanges.

"GAB" Custom Token - TBA

Decentralized; Transparent; Secure

Gable liquidity protocol is non-custodial. Liquidity pools and lending products are governed by the laws of smart contracts. Gable's smart contracts are designed so that only the rightful owner can claim their portion of the pool's liquidity.

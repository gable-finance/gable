Stake and Earn Interest, or Borrow

Sundae is a decentralized liquidity market protocol built on Radix DLT, where users can participate as borrowers or suppliers. Version 1 - 15 October 2023

Sundae's first iteration of Sundae liquidity protocol will be released shortly after the Radix Babylon launch. This release will consist of the following features:

  • Validator node

    • A validator node will be deployed that collects staking rewards, and deposits it in a liquidity pool on behalf of the staker.

  • Liquidity pool

    • The first liquidity pool will collect liquidity via staking rewards. A novel approach to provide liquidity, that enables liquidity providers to leverage on their staking rewards. This offers unique benefits, especially at Babylon launch when the staking yield (APY) will be relatively high.

  • Flash loan

    • The first offered lending product will be flash loans. Flash loans offer instant liquidity and the ability to lower spreads across exchanges. Bringing these features into the Radix ecosystem at an early stage will contribute to Radix's succes and ability to grow.

Version 1+

In the remainder of 2023, after the launch of Sundae Version 1, Sundae will prioritize the following initiatives:

  • User adoption

    • By utilizing social media channels (Twitter, Telegram, Youtube, etc.) and improving the user experience.

  • Increased liquidity

    • This will be achieved by promoting Sundae's validator node to XRD holders.

Version 2, ... , n

Subsequent iterations, taking place in 2024, will expand the protocol on the following aspects (in no particular order):

  • Sundae governance/utility token
  • Lending products
  • Supported (stable) tokens
  • Liquidity pools and methods for providing liquidity

Decentralized; Transparent; Secure

Sundae liquidity protocol is non-custodial. Liquidity pools and lending products are governed by the laws of smart contracts. Sundae's smart contracts are designed so that only the rightful owner can claim their portion of the pool's liquidity.

Liquidity protocol on Radix DLT

Stake and Earn Interest, or Borrow

Sundae is a decentralized liquidity market protocol build on Radix DLT where users can participate as borrower or supplier. The protocol offers lending products for borrowers, and liquidity solutions for suppliers. Built on Radix DLT, the protocol offers transparency, security, and innovative solutions to traders and investors. This allows users to enjoy fast and efficient transaction processing, low fees, and transparent terms.

This is how it works:

[1] Stake XRD

The first step into becoming a liquidity provider, is to stake XRD at Sundae's validator node. In return you will receive 'Liquid staking units' (LSU's) - which serve as proof of your stake.

The validator node claims the staking rewards on your behalve, and deposits them automatically into the liquidity pool.

[2] Earn Interest

The by you provided liquidity is used to issue lending products (flash loans) to users of the protocol. The fee's collected from this service are allocated proportionally to you.

[3] Claim Resources

Finally, once you decide to withdraw your liquidity, the NFT allows you to instantly and at any time claim:

  • LSU's that you have deposited to the protocol
  • Staking rewards that you are entitled to
  • Interest earned by the liquidity pool

Subsequently these LSU's allow you to unstake and rerurn your staked XRD.

Access liquidity instantly; anywhere!

Borrow at Sundae's:

Sundae Liquidity Protocol currently offers flash loans as its main feature. Flash loans enable you to take out loans without collateral that are executed within a single transaction. Liquidity can be accessed instantly and at low interest rates, to make profitable trades or take advantage of market inefficiencies. This allows anyone to participate in DeFi markets in a more accessible and cost-effective way. Overall, Sundae's flash loan feature offers a powerful tool for financial innovation and market participation.

Version 1 - 13 August 2023

Sundae's first iteration of Sundae liquidity protocol will be released shortly after the Radix Babylon launch. This release will consist of the following features:

  • Validator node

    • A validator node will be deployed that collects staking rewards, and deposits it in a liquidity pool on behalve of the staker.

  • Liquidity pool

    • The first liquidity pool will collect liquidity via staking rewards. A novel approach to provide liquidity, that enables liquidity providers to leverage on their staking rewards. This offers unique benefits, especially at Babylon launch when the staking yield (APY) will be relatively high.

  • Flash loan

    • The first offered lending product will be flash loans. Flash loans offer instant liquidity and the ability to lower spreads accross exchanges. Bringing these features into the Radix ecosystem at an early stage will contribute to Radix's succes and ability to grow.

Version 1+

The remainder of 2023, after launch of Sundae Version 1, Sundae will lay priority on the following:

  • User adoption

    • By utilizing social media channels (Twitter, Telegram, Youtube, etc.) and improving the user experience.

  • Increased liquidity

    • This will be achieved by promoting Sundae's validator node to XRD holders.

Version 2, ... , n

Subsequent iterations, taking place in 2024, will expand the protocol on the following aspects (in no particular order):

  • Native governance/utility token
  • Lending products
  • Supported tokens
  • Liquidity pools and methods to provide liquidity

Decentral; Transparant; Secure

Sundae liquidity protocol is non-custodial. Liquidity pools and lending products are governed by the laws of smart contracts. Sundae's smart contracts are designed so that only the rightfull owner can lay claim on their portion of the pool's liquidity.

Sundae Liquidity Protocol

Introducing a revolutionary liquidity protocol built on Radix DLT, which currently offers flash loans as its main feature. This decentralized protocol enables users to borrow funds without collateral, provided that the borrowed funds are returned within the same transaction. This unique feature allows users to access liquidity for trading, arbitrage, and other use cases, without requiring them to put up their own funds as collateral. The flash loans feature is just one example of the innovative lending products that the protocol will offer, making it an ideal platform for traders and investors looking for a cutting-edge and secure liquidity solution.

Built on a peer-to-peer network, the protocol offers transparency, security, and trust among all participants. With the power of Radix DLT, this protocol has the potential to transform the way assets are traded and exchanged.

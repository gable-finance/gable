Privacy Notice

Effective Date: 01 October 2023

Introduction

This Privacy Notice describes how we process information received during your interactions with our website. This notice may be amended from time to time, and new versions will be published on our website, which can be found at gable.finance/privacy-policy.

Gable Finance

This Notice is issued by Gable Finance Radix and applies to Gable Finance. Gable Finance is registered under the Data Protection (Jersey) Law 2018 (the “Law”) and will process personal data in accordance with the provisions of the Law. Gable Finance is committed to protecting your privacy and personal data. This Privacy Notice describes the personal data that we collect, the purposes for which we use such data, the steps we take to store and protect it and your choices regarding our use of it.

Personal Data

Personal data is any information held on a data subject. This includes information such as the subject’s name, address and contact details and may include their IP addresses and on-line identifiers. It may include information on physical, physiological, mental, economic, and social identity factors. A Data Subject is an identifiable living individual to whom personal data relates. Personal data includes information processed or held electronically e.g. on a computer and/or non-electronically e.g. paper records held in a filing system.

What Personal Data do we Collect?

We do not collect any personal data of you.

What is Sensitive Data?

Sensitive data is data defined in the Law as such e.g., racial, or ethnic origin, political opinion, religion, trade union membership, health data, genetic or biometric data or criminal records. We do not collect any sensitive data of you.

Web3 Account Information

We do not store personal data. However, we may collect and store web3 account-related information as part of your interaction with our website. This may include account addresses, transaction history, and other relevant web3 data.

Use of Web3 Account Information

The web3 account-related information collected is solely used for the purpose of providing you with access to and facilitating interactions with our web3-based services. We do not use this information for any other purposes, such as marketing or analytics.

How does Gable Finance Collect Data?

We collect and process data that you provide to us directly (or indirectly via your advisors) when you make use of our services.

Cookies

Cookies are pieces of information that your web browser transfers to your computer's hard drive for record-keeping purposes. They can be used to store information about your preferences on a particular website. Most browsers are initially set to accept cookies, but you can set your browser to refuse them if you want to. Gable.finance may use cookies to enhance your experience and provide more efficient services.

Data Protection

We regard the lawful and appropriate treatment of your web3 account-related information as fundamental to our successful operation and maintaining trust between gable.finance and its users. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to secure this data against unauthorized or unlawful access, processing, loss, or destruction.

Your Rights

While we do not store personal data, you have the right to access and manage your web3 account-related information. You can exercise these rights through your web3 provider or the web3 account management tools provided by gable.finance.

Contacting Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or inquiries regarding our privacy practices or the data we collect, please contact us at contact@gable.finance.

Boost your Earnings; Provide Liquidity with Ease!

Here's how you do it:

[1] Stake XRD at Sundae validator node

The first step into supplying liquidity is to stake your XRD at Sundae validator node. In return you will receive 'Liquid staking units' (LSU's) - which serve as proof of your stake.

Note: as you probably noticed, the validator node fee is set at 100%. This means that 100% of the staking rewards are distributed to the validator's wallet. This wallet represents the liquidity pool. All rewards that you are entitled to are registered by Sundae and can be collected at any time by you, from the liquidity pool. In essence the validator node fee is 0%, as 100% of the staking rewards are allocated to the liquidity supplier (i.e. staker) via the liquidity pool. More on this in the next section.

                    [2] Deposit LSU's into Sundae's liquidity pool
                

                

                    The second step is to deposit the Sundae validator node LSU's into the liquidity pool smart contract. 
                    By doing so, you register yourself as liquidity provided.
                    Sundae's smart contract will now administrate the staking rewards and interest earnings that you earn over time.
                


                

                    In exchange to your LSU deposit, you receive a non-fungible token (NFT). 
                    Inside the data of this NFT, the amount of LSU's that you are entitled to is registered.
                    This NFT serves as your proof of supply.
                    With this NFT you can claim all the resources that you are entitled to (see step 3).

                    Collecting liquidity via staking rewards
                

                

                    The staking rewards obtained by the validator node are utilized as liquidity for the protocol. 
                    Sundae records the entitled staking rewards for each staker, which can be claimed by the staker at any time. 
                    For more details, refer to section [3].
                
    
                    
                

                    The collected liquidity is utilized to issue lending products, such as flash loans, which generate interest.
                    Fifty percent of the accrued interest is distributed to the liquidity suppliers, proportionally based on their stake in the liquidity pool.
                    Once the liquidity supplier chooses to unstake, the accumulated interest, along with the staking rewards, is transferred to the respective wallet associated with the supplier.                
                


                

                


            


            

                

                    Payout scheme
                

                
    
                    
  • Staking rewards: 100% to supplier
    • 
                

               
                

                    Liquidity suppliers are entitled to 100% of the staking rewards. These can be claimed from the liquidity pool.
                

                
    
                    
  • Interest earnings: 50% supplier; 50% Sundae
    • 
                

                
                

                    Interest earnings are divided equally between the liquidity supplier and Sundae.
                

                
            


            

                

                    Sundae liquidity protocol is non-custodial. 
                    Liquidity pools and lending products are governed by the laws of smart contracts. 
                    Sundae's smart contracts are designed in a manner that only the rightfull owner can claim the pool's liquidity.
                

                
            


            

                

                    [3] Claim resources
                

                

                    Finally, withdrawing your liquidity and claiming your earnings can be done at any point in time by unstaking XRD from the validator node. 
                    Claiming your resources can be achieved by returning Sundae validator node's LSU's.
                    This returns the following resources to your wallet:
                

                
    
                    
  • LSU's that you have deposited into the liquidity pool
    • 
                    
  • Staking rewards (XRD) that you are entitled to
    • 
                    
  • Interest earnings (XRD) that you are entitled to
    • 
                

                

                    Once your LSU's are returned to your wallet you can use these to withdraw your stake from the Sundae validator node.
                    This takes 500 epochs (approx. 2 weeks) to return, as is inherent to the Radix network.

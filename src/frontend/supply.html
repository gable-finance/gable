Boost your Earnings; Provide Liquidity with Ease!

Gable Liquidity Protocol collects liquidity via staking rewards. Before you get started as supplier it is advised to read the documentation that is available for suppliers on Gable.

[1] Stake XRD at Gable validator node

The first step into supplying liquidity is to stake your XRD at Gable validator node. In return you will receive 'Liquid staking units' (LSU's) - which serve as proof of your stake.

                    [2] Deposit LSU's into Gable's liquidity pool
                

                

                    The second step is to deposit the Gable validator node LSU's into the liquidity pool smart contract. 
                    By doing so, you register yourself as liquidity provided.
                


                

                    In exchange to your LSU deposit, you receive a non-fungible token (NFT). 
                    With this NFT you can claim all the resources that you are entitled to (see step 3).
